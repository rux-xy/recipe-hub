Caprese Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 20 cherry tomatoes
  • 20 small fresh mozzarella balls
  • 20 fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 20 small skewers

Steps:

  1. Wash and dry the cherry tomatoes and basil leaves
  2. Thread one cherry tomato, one basil leaf, and one mozzarella ball onto each skewer
  3. Arrange the skewers on a serving platter
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  6. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve

Garlic Herb Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette, sliced into ½-inch pieces
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic, whole (for rubbing)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Arrange baguette slices on a baking sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes until lightly toasted
  3. In a bowl, combine diced tomatoes, minced garlic, basil, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar
  4. Season the tomato mixture with salt and pepper
  5. Rub each toasted baguette slice with a whole garlic clove
  6. Spoon the tomato mixture onto the toasted bread slices
  7. Serve immediately
